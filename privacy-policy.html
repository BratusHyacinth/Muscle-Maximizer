Privacy Policy

Muscle Maximizer  ·  Last updated: May 15, 2026

The short version: Muscle Maximizer does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. Your workout history and health data stay on your device — or in your private iCloud account. We never see any of it.

Who We Are

Muscle Maximizer is an independent iOS app developed by Michael Kolovos. If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at mkolovos327@gmail.com.

Data We Do Not Collect

Muscle Maximizer does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, device ID)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports sent to a third-party server
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Payment information

The app contains no third-party analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and makes no network requests to any developer-controlled server.

Workout & Training Data

All workout data you import — including exercises, sets, reps, weights, and dates — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is never transmitted to any external server, including ours.

If you have iCloud enabled, this data syncs across your Apple devices through your private CloudKit container. This means the data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is accessible only to you. We do not have access to your CloudKit data. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app's Settings.

Apple Health (HealthKit) Data

Muscle Maximizer can optionally read heart rate and calorie data from Apple Health to improve recovery estimates. This integration is entirely optional. If you choose not to grant access, the app functions fully using research-based defaults.

When HealthKit access is granted:

  • The app requests read-only access to heart rate and active energy data during workout windows.
  • HealthKit data is used solely to compute an Exertion Score for each session and is never stored independently of that calculation.
  • HealthKit data is never written to iCloud, never uploaded to any server, and never shared with any third party — in compliance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines (App Store Review Guideline 5.1.3).

You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health on your device.

iCloud Sync

Workout history, exercise settings, and recovery preferences are synced via Apple's CloudKit using a private database zone. This means:

  • Data is tied to your Apple ID and stored in your personal iCloud account.
  • No Muscle Maximizer developer account or server is involved in the sync process.
  • Apple's iCloud privacy policy governs the storage of this data on Apple's infrastructure.

You can disable iCloud sync entirely in Muscle Maximizer → Settings → iCloud Sync. Your data will remain on-device only.

No Account Required

Muscle Maximizer does not require you to create an account, register an email address, or log in. The app works entirely without any form of authentication beyond your existing Apple ID (used solely for iCloud sync if enabled).

No Advertising

Muscle Maximizer contains no advertisements and does not use advertising networks or tracking identifiers of any kind. The app is free with no in-app purchases and no sponsored content.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data created in Muscle Maximizer is under your control. To delete your data:

  • On-device data: Delete the app from your iPhone or iPad. All local data is removed immediately.
  • iCloud data: Go to Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage, find Muscle Maximizer, and delete its data. Alternatively, deleting the app will remove the associated CloudKit data over time per Apple's retention policies.
  • HealthKit access: Revoke permissions at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Muscle Maximizer.

Children's Privacy

Muscle Maximizer is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because the app collects no personal data from any user, there is no specific risk to minors.
