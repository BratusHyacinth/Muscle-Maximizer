Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import a workout?

Open the Import tab and paste your workout text, or use the Share Sheet in Strong or Hevy to send a workout directly to Muscle Maximizer. The app auto-detects the format and shows a preview before saving.

Which apps can I import from?

Muscle Maximizer supports Strong (legacy and modern formats), Hevy, and generic plain-text or CSV workout exports. If your app isn't recognized, email us with a sample export and we'll add support.

My muscle recovery percentages look off. How are they calculated?

Recovery is calculated using research-backed baseline windows (36–96 hours depending on the muscle group), adjusted for your session's volume and intensity relative to your personal history. If something seems wrong, check Settings → Recovery Baselines and make sure your recent workouts are imported correctly. You can also customize the baseline hours per muscle group.

How do I connect Apple Health?

Go to Settings → Apple Health and tap "Connect". The app requests read-only access to heart rate and active calories to calculate an Exertion Score. You can revoke this at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Muscle Maximizer.

My workouts aren't syncing across my devices.

Make sure iCloud is enabled in Settings → [your name] → iCloud and that Muscle Maximizer is toggled on. Also check Settings inside the app to confirm iCloud Sync is turned on. Sync can take a few minutes after a new import.

How do I delete all my data?

Deleting the app removes all on-device data immediately. To also remove your iCloud data, go to iOS Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage, find Muscle Maximizer, and delete its data before uninstalling.

Is my data private?

Yes. Muscle Maximizer contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and makes no network requests except direct iCloud sync to Apple's servers. Your data is never seen by the developer. Read the full Privacy Policy for details.

Is Muscle Maximizer really free?

Yes — every feature is available from day one. There are no paywalls, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.
